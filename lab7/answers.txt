- 2. 
My timing results were:
warmup(5) calculated -1599990000000000 in  0.00 ns/elt
dot(arr1, arr2, LENGTH) calculated      -6482137000 in  0.29 ns/elt
dot_c(arr1, arr2, LENGTH) calculated      -6482137000 in  0.22 ns/elt
dot_struct(arr_pair, LENGTH) calculated      -6482137000 in  0.29 ns/elt
dot_struct_c(arr_pair, LENGTH) calculated      -6482137000 in  0.29 ns/elt
The C dot function was the fastest. My assembly dot function was a little slower. The assembly struct version and the C struct version were almost the same speed.
In general, the C compiler optimized the normal array dot product better than my simple assembly loop.
